Hobbies

Ever since I was young, I have enjoyed playing historical strategy games on the computer. The combination of acting out real events from history and devising a winning strategy to achieve the objectives is almost always fascinating.

For a year, I was the treasurer for the university anime society.

When I have the time, I like to exercise my cooking skills, trying out new dishes and then putting my own personal twist on them.

When I’m not inside, I enjoy doing outdoor activities, whether it is playing football with friends and colleagues, going to the gym after work, hiking with family, or simply going for a relaxing walk by myself.

When I have the opportunity, I adore travelling abroad, exploring other countries and their cultures.

I am a keen follower of current affairs and politics in particular. Though the fast pace of events lately has hampered my ability to keep up in the same amount of depth as I did previously, I still like to make sure I’m up to date on what’s going on around the world.
